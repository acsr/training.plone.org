Mastering Plone!
================

Mastering Plone is intended as a week-long training for people who are new to Plone or want to learn about the current best practices of Plone development.

About
-----

The training was created by Philip Bauer and Patrick Gerken of `starzel.de <http://www.starzel.de>`_ to create a canonical training for future Plone developers. The aim is that anyone with the appropriate knowledge can give a training based on it and contribute to it. It is published as Open Source on `github <https://github.com/plone/training>`_ and `training.plone.org <http://training.plone.org/>`_.

Read more at http://training.plone.org/5/about.html
